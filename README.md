# Skill2Salary


Problem Statement

A large number of students and young graduates encounter difficulties in choosing the appropriate 
skillsets and career direction due to the non availability of proper guidance. In most cases, decisions are 
being made considering social media trends and opinions instead of factual data on the job market. As a 
result of this, youngsters, including students, end up choosing improper skillsets, which result in poor 
job prospects, career development, and overall confidence levels.
At the moment, there is no platform that effectively links a student’s skills, education, experience, and 
location with salary and employment outcomes. This is a challenge that makes it difficult for students to 
decide on their future.
Hence, there is a need for a data-driven system, which can help guide students towards skills in-demand 
and more career advancement opportunities.


Solution

Skill2Salary is an AI-based tool that enhances students by offering personalized insights about their 
potential future career prospects. The tool creates a profile of users by collecting information such as 
skills, education, and experience through a simple interface.
This information is processed through a Java-based backend system and real job market datasets in 
order to predict the salary range and job success probability. Skill gaps are also identified along with 
relevant skills in order to help users become employable.
By doing so, Skill2Salary enables students to study based on user skills in terms of probable career 
outcomes, allowing them to make informed decisions and focus on popular technologies prior to 
effectively planning their careers. Moreover, the solution is free of charge, which makes it accessible to 
all students regardless of their background.
